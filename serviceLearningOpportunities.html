Robotics Organization

The HS Robotics Team is looking for volunteers to help label and organize the robotics equipment storage. It will also require creativity to find unique solutions to the storage challenges faced. This volunteer opportunity can be scheduled with Mr. Bennett at jonathan.bennett@sjajeju.kr.


STEAM Faire

The CS Honor Society is looking for volunteers to help plan and design our interactive displays at the STEAM Faire. Previous displays have included:

  • High 5 Cam
  • Fruit Piano
  • Co-op Dinosaur game

We are looking for people with a creative side who can plan and design the display. It should look good! Coding experience is not required. Please contact Mr. Elliot at matthew.elliot@sjajeju.kr if you are interested.
